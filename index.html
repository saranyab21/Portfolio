Improving Brain Tumor Classification using Semi-Supervised Latent Space Learning

The study tackles the scarcity of labeled data in training deep learning models for brain tumor classification from MRI images. The approach employs a Variational Autoencoder for latent representation learning and utilizes a semi-supervised strategy, combining a small labeled set with a larger pool of unlabeled data through self-training. Evaluation on binary and multi-class tumor datasets demonstrates competitive performance compared to fully-supervised baselines and state-of-the-art semi-supervised frameworks.

View

Pneumonic - Transfer Learning-based Pneumonia Detection using Chest X-ray images

The study presents a CNN-based transfer learning approach using different pre-trained models (Resnet50, DenseNet, VGG16, InceptionV3) to detect pediatric pneumonia and classify bacterial and viral pneumonia with higher accuracy. With exceptional Recall (99.23%) and F1 Score (92.53%), the approach accelerates diagnosis, refining clinical outcomes by promptly identifying treatable conditions.

CommuteLink - A Ride Sharing Platform designed for corporate ecosystems

Unveiling a paradigm shift in employee mobility, the platform integrates personal vehicle registration, catalyzing efficient pooling at a nominal cost. Motorists effortlessly schedule rides upon vehicle registration, while riders enjoy the ease of intuitive booking and incident reporting. At the same time, admin oversight ensures security, allowing only approved vehicles in this transformative mobility solution.

PayOpus- Employee Payroll Management System

The Java Swing-based payroll system is a user-friendly GUI application designed to streamline employee payment processes. It grants exclusive data access to administrators for enhanced efficiency, storing comprehensive employee information, managing salaries, and automating wage calculations based on workdays.
